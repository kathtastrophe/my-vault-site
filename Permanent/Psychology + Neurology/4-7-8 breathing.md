# 4 - 7 - 8 Breathing

Use this technique to calm [[anxiety]].

**Breathe in** through your nose to a count of 4.

**Hold** for a count of 7.

**Breathe out** slowly through your mouth for a count of 8.

**Repeat** 4 times.
